Incident: Memory Leak in OmniSLAM EKF

STATUS: RESOLVED | DATE: 2025-01-14 | TOOL: Valgrind

During long-duration mapping tests, the ROS node crashed after ~45 minutes. Analysis revealed that the `PoseGraph` optimization loop was pushing new constraints to a `std::vector` without clearing the temporary residuals from the previous iteration.

Line 142: constraints_.push_back(new_constraint); // <-- LEAK: Never cleared old pointers
Fix: Implemented RAII wrapper and explicit `clear()` after optimization pass.
